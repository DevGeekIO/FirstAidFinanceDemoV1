Whether you're starting a new practice from the ground up, buying into an existing partnership, or renovating your current clinic space, First Aid Finance provides customised finance solutions to meet your specific needs. What We Can Fund

Our relationships with specialist medical lenders allow us to secure funding for a wide range of practice needs, often without requiring residential property as security.

  • Practice Purchase: Up to 100% finance available for goodwill and assets.
  • Fit-out & Renovation: Escrow facilities to manage payments to builders.
  • Commercial Property: Up to 90% LVR for owner-occupied medical suites (industry specific).
  • Working Capital: Overdrafts to manage cash flow gaps during the start-up phase.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to own a home to get a practice loan?
Not necessarily. While owning a home strengthens your application, many Medico-specific lenders allow borrowing against the "Goodwill" or cash flow of the practice itself, sometimes up to 100% of the purchase price.
How long does the approval process take?
For a standard practice purchase, approval generally takes 3-4 weeks. However, complex structures or commercial property purchases may take 6-8 weeks. We recommend starting the conversation as soon as you identify an opportunity.
Can you help with equipment finance too?
Yes. We can often bundle equipment finance (for chairs, X-ray machines, IT systems) with your practice loan, or set up a separate facility to keep your cash flow free.
What LVR can I get for commercial premises?
Standard commercial loans are usually capped at 65-70%. However, for owner-occupied medical suites, we have access to lenders who offer up to 90% LVR, often without Lenders Mortgage Insurance (LMI).
